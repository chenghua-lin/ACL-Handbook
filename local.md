## Overview of Responsibilities

The Local Organization Chairs at *CL conferences are responsible for overseeing and coordinating all of the meeting, accomodation, entertainment and eating arrangements.\
\
**The following are the main responsibilities of the Local Chairs:**

**1. Arrange Conference Venue**\
**2. Social Events & Optional Visitor Activities**

**It is recommended that the Local Organization Chair works with a professional organizer, the cost of which should be incorporated into the conference budget.**

For information regarding writing conference bids pre-acceptance, see [here](https://acl-org.github.io/conference-handbook/bid_guidelines/).
